## 🎨 Visual Design Specs

### Color Scheme:
- **Growth Engine column:** Green/Gold (winning column)
- **Competitor columns:** Gray/Blue (standard)
- **Checkmarks:** Green ✅
- **X marks:** Red ❌
- **Warning signs:** Yellow ⚠️

### Typography:
- **Headline:** Bold, 48px
- **Feature names:** 18px semi-bold
- **Cell text:** 14px regular
- **Price:** 24px bold

### Icons/Graphics:
- Use checkmarks, X's, and warning triangles
- Add small icons for each feature (phone, calendar, website, chart)
- Trophy or ribbon icon on Growth Engine column header

### Dimensions:
- **Social media:** 1080px × 1350px (Instagram portrait)
- **Website:** 1200px × 800px (landscape)
- **Presentation:** 1920px × 1080px (16:9)
