## 📱 How the AI Handles Different Call Types

### Scenario 1: New Client Calling to Book
**Caller:** "Hi, I'd like to schedule an appointment."

**AI:**
1. Greets them warmly
2. Asks what service they need
3. Checks if they're a new or returning client
4. Collects name, phone, email
5. Checks availability in real-time
6. Books the appointment
7. Sends confirmation text

**Result:** Appointment booked in under 2 minutes, lead captured

---

### Scenario 2: Pricing/Info Questions
**Caller:** "How much does [service] cost?"

**AI:**
1. Provides price range from your config
2. Explains what's included
3. Offers free consultation (if applicable)
4. Asks if they'd like to schedule

**Result:** Lead educated, opportunity to book

---

### Scenario 3: Emergency Situation
**Caller:** "[Industry-specific emergency]" (e.g., "My tooth is killing me", "My AC is out", "I was in a car accident")

**AI:**
1. Flags call as urgent
2. Reassures the caller
3. Gets their contact info
4. Books emergency/priority slot
5. Sends alert to you immediately

**Result:** Emergency handled professionally, customer reassured

---

### Scenario 4: After-Hours Call
**Caller:** Calls at 11pm

**AI:**
1. Acknowledges call outside business hours
2. Offers to book for next available time
3. Captures lead info for morning callback
4. Sends confirmation text

**Result:** Lead captured even while you sleep
