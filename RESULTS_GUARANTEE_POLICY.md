## 📋 Guarantee Terms & Conditions

### **Eligibility Requirements:**

To qualify for the guarantee, client must:

1. **Receive Minimum Call Volume:**
   - At least 20 inbound calls during the 30-day period
   - Calls must be legitimate business inquiries (not spam/wrong numbers)
   - **Rationale:** Can't book appointments if phone doesn't ring

2. **Have Bookable Services:**
   - Client must offer services that can be booked via phone
   - Pricing must be disclosed (AI can discuss rates)
   - Availability must exist (calendar has open slots)
   - **Rationale:** Can't book if nothing is available to book

3. **Cooperate with Setup:**
   - Complete onboarding call within first week
   - Provide required business information
   - Connect calendar for booking
   - Respond to our support requests within 48 hours
   - **Rationale:** We need their cooperation to set up correctly

4. **Use System as Primary Answering:**
   - Forward main business line to AI receptionist
   - Not used as "backup" or "overflow" only
   - **Rationale:** Can't prove value if we only get leftover calls

### **Exclusions (When Guarantee Does NOT Apply):**

Guarantee is void if:

- ❌ Client receives fewer than 20 legitimate calls in 30 days (not our fault)
- ❌ Client doesn't connect calendar or make availability for bookings
- ❌ Client's services require in-person assessment only (unbookable by phone)
- ❌ Client disconnects AI or stops forwarding calls before 30 days
- ❌ Client fails to complete onboarding or provide required info
- ❌ Force majeure (natural disaster, client's business closes, etc.)

### **What Counts as "Booked Lead":**

A "booked lead" is defined as:

✅ **Counts:**
- Appointment scheduled via AI receptionist (confirmed in calendar)
- Client inquiry that resulted in follow-up appointment
- Consultation booked (even if it's free consultation)
- Service call scheduled (for home services, etc.)

❌ **Doesn't Count:**
- Information-only calls (caller just wanted pricing, didn't book)
- Wrong numbers, spam calls, robocalls
- Existing clients calling to reschedule (not a NEW booking)
- Appointments client booked themselves (not via our AI)

## 🔍 Verification Process

### **How We Track Results:**

1. **Call Logs:**
   - All calls are recorded and transcribed (with client consent)
   - We can review every conversation
   - Timestamp and outcome tracked in dashboard

2. **Calendar Integration:**
   - Direct access to client's Google Calendar
   - We can see appointments that were added
   - Can correlate calls → bookings

3. **Client Dashboard:**
   - Client has real-time view of all calls
   - Can see which calls resulted in bookings
   - Transparent reporting

### **Refund Request Process:**

If client believes they qualify for refund:

1. **Client submits refund request** (email to support@scalewithjak.com)
2. **We review call logs** within 3 business days
3. **Verify criteria:**
   - Did client receive 20+ legitimate calls?
   - Were any appointments booked?
   - Did client meet eligibility requirements?
4. **Decision communicated** within 5 business days
5. **Refund processed** (if approved) within 7-10 business days

### **Dispute Resolution:**

If client disagrees with our assessment:

- We provide call-by-call breakdown showing why calls didn't book
- Client can request independent review of call recordings
- Final decision by company leadership within 10 days
- If still disputed, client can initiate credit card chargeback
