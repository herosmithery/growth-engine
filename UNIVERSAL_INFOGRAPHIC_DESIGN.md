## 📐 Design Specifications

### Color Palette (Business-Agnostic, Professional)
- **Primary:** Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) - Trust, professionalism
- **Secondary:** Vibrant Green (#10B981) - Growth, success
- **Accent:** Bright Orange (#F59E0B) - Energy, urgency
- **Background:** Light Gray (#F9FAFB) - Clean, modern
- **Text:** Dark Gray (#1F2937) - Readability

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, Sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, or Poppins)
- **Body Text:** Regular, Sans-serif (Inter or Roboto)
- **Stats/Numbers:** Extra Bold, Large

### Icons
- Use modern, flat-style icons
- Consistent stroke width
- Industry-agnostic where possible (phone, calendar, checkmark, etc.)
- Industry-specific only in headline area
