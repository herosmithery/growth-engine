## 🎤 KEY OBJECTION HANDLING

### **"How much does it cost?"**
✅ **Don't quote price on discovery call**
✅ **Do:** Defer to full discovery call, mention ROI, compare to competitors
✅ **Close:** "Can we hop on a 15-min call so you see what this looks like for YOUR business?"

### **"I already use [Competitor]"**
✅ **Don't trash-talk competitor**
✅ **Do:** Validate their choice, position as evolution/upgrade
✅ **Close:** "A lot of our clients started there and upgraded. Worth a call to see if that fits you?"

### **"I'm too busy"**
✅ **Don't push harder**
✅ **Do:** Use their objection as the solution
✅ **Close:** "That's exactly why people come to us — so you STOP being the bottleneck. 15 minutes?"

### **"I need to think about it"**
✅ **Don't let them ghost**
✅ **Do:** Reframe as "let's just see if it's a fit first"
✅ **Close:** "No pitch, no pressure. Just a real look at what's leaking. Fair?"

## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ❌ **Don't Do This:**

1. **Quote pricing before discovery call**
   - Kills conversion, gives them a reason to say no

2. **Trash-talk competitors**
   - Makes you look insecure, defensive

3. **Over-pitch features**
   - Goal is to book call, not close on phone

4. **Let call end without next step**
   - Every call should end with booked slot or clear disqualification

5. **Sound like you're reading a script**
   - Natural conversation > robotic script reading

### ✅ **Do This Instead:**

1. **Qualify first, book second**
   - Make sure they're a fit before pushing for calendar

2. **Mirror their energy**
   - Busy person = be brief. Curious person = explore more.

3. **Validate objections**
   - "I hear you" > "But here's why you're wrong"

4. **Create curiosity, not saturation**
   - Less is more. Leave them wanting to learn more on discovery call.

5. **Sound human**
   - Use natural transitions: "Got it," "Makes sense," "Fair enough"
